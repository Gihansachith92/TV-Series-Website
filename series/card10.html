The 100

7.5 IMDb | 2014 43 min | Drama | Sci-Fi | Mystery


Set 97 years after a nuclear apocalypse, on-board a dying space station running out of air.
One hundred juvenile delinquents are sent back to Earth to see if it is habitable.
On the ground they will be tested by the Earth, the secrets it hides and, most of all, themselves.
